What's Involved

Over time, every phone battery loses its ability to hold a charge — that's normal. But when you're constantly tethered to a charger or your phone shuts down at 20%, it's time for a replacement. We fit high-capacity replacement batteries across all major brands. The job takes under an hour and your phone will hold charge like it did on day one.

Signs You Need This Repair

  • Battery draining much faster than it used to
  • Phone shutting down unexpectedly at low percentages
  • Battery percentage jumping around
  • Phone gets unusually hot when charging or in use

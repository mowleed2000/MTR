What's Involved

A faulty charging port is one of the most frustrating phone problems — especially when you don't realise that's the issue. If your phone charges slowly, only works at a certain angle, or doesn't respond to the charger at all, the port is likely the problem. We clean, repair or replace charging ports across all phone models. Most jobs are done the same day.

Signs You Need This Repair

  • Charger only works at a certain angle
  • Phone charges very slowly or intermittently
  • Charger feels loose or falls out easily
  • Phone shows no response when plugged in
